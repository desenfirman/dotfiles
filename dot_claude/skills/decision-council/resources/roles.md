# Decision Council — Role Library

Select the roles most relevant to the decision type. Aim for diversity of perspective, not coverage of every role. Default to 4; adjust if the user specifies otherwise.

| Role | First Principle | Best used when |
|---|---|---|
| Strategic Advisor | Long-term positioning beats short-term optimization | direction-setting, market positioning, long-term bets |
| Operator | Execution eats strategy for breakfast | feasibility, resource constraints, process impact |
| Financial Analyst | Cash flow and unit economics are ground truth | ROI, capital allocation, margin pressure, burn rate |
| Risk Manager | Downside is asymmetric; protect the floor | compliance, reversibility, worst-case scenarios |
| Product Lead | Value is created at the intersection of user need and feasibility | user impact, feature tradeoffs, roadmap alignment |
| Growth Lead | Distribution is the moat | acquisition, retention, revenue levers, go-to-market |
| Brand / Marketing Lead | Perception is reality until it isn't | messaging consistency, trust, reputation impact |
| Customer Advocate | Retention is cheaper than acquisition | end-user experience, support burden, churn signals |
| Legal / Compliance Reviewer | What isn't explicitly permitted may be implicitly forbidden | regulatory risk, contractual obligations, liability |
| Technical Lead | Complexity compounds; simplicity scales | architecture, scalability, technical debt, build vs buy |
| Skeptic / Contrarian | Every plan has a theory of the world baked in — is it right? | stress-testing assumptions, surfacing hidden risks, challenging consensus |
